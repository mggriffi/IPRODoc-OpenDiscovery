Active Learning Status

Active Learning Status indicates the overall completeness of training on the Active Learning Enabled Review Pass:

Active Learning Status indicates how much training has been applied to the Active Learning Review Pass and helps to indicate when further review might not be required. This status also indicates how accurate the Precision and Recall numbers are in Review Performance. Until reaching a "Good" or "Excellent" status, training is insufficient to obtain a really accurate Precision and Recall.

Poor - Insufficient positive reviewed documents to make predictions.

Mediocre - More examples of positive reviewed documents are required to improve predictions.

Good - More examples of positive reviewed documents are recommended to improve predictions. Positive predicted documents should be prioritized into review batches.

Excellent - Sufficient training to effectively predict and prioritize documents in to review. Additional review is recommended to improve precision and recall.

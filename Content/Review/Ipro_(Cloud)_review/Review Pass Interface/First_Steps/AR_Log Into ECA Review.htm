Log into ECA Review

Before logging into ECA Review, obtain the following information from your administrator:

  • URL

  • user name and password

To log on to :

  1. Start your web browser.

  2. Enter or navigate to the URL defined for .

  3. On the Login screen, enter the credentials provided by your administrator.

  4. Optional: Select the Remember me option.

  5. Click Login. The Home page appears.

  6. Click ECA & Review on the Home page to open the Review home page.

Note: A user may have multiple log on sessions across the platform, but only one Review session is permitted.

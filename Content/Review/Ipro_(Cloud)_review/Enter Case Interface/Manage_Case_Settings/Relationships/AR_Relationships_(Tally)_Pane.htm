Relationships Panel

In , document relationships include the following types of information for each document:

  • Document history: Record of the various actions taken on the document, such as tagging and field edits.

  • Family documents: Based on the content of the BEGATTACH field. The exact details provided in for this relationship are configured by your administrator.

  • Near Duplicates and/or Email Threads: Your administrator may configure either or both of these types of relationships.

  • Duplicate documents: Based on the content of the MD5HASH field. The exact details provided in for this relationship are configured by your administrator.

  • Similar Documents: Allows documents deemed similar to those documents included through other search relationships.

This section explains how to view the relationship information that exists for the documents in your case.

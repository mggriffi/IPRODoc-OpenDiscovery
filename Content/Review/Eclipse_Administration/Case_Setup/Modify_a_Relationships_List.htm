Modify a Relationships List

To modify or delete a Relationships list:

  1. Get started:

    1. Start Eclipse and log in as an Eclipse administrator.

    2. Open the case for which relationships are being defined.

    3. In the Case View pane, click Case Administration and then Relationships.

  2. In the list of relationships, click the one to be modified. For tips on working in lists, see Use Lists.

  3. To delete the selected relationships, click , then click Yes in response to the confirmation message.

  4. To revise the relationship definition, click  and make needed changes. Refer to Create a Relationship List for details on the relationship configuration.

  5. When finished, click Save.

  6. Notify users of the changes that have been made.

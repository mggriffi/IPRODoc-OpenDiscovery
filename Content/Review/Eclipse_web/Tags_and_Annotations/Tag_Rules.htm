Tag Rules

The following rules can be applied to document tag groups by your administrator. (Tag rules do not apply to page tags.)

Field

Description

Exclusive

Only one tag in the group can be applied to a document.

Required

At least one tag in the group must be applied to all documents in a batch before a batch can be marked complete.

Locked

Maintain the current state of tags in the group. That is, if a tag in the group has been applied to documents, it remains in this state. If a tag has not been applied to documents, it cannot be applied.

Tag Entire Family

When a tag in the group is applied to a document, the tag will be applied to the selected document and its relatives as explained in "Tags and Related Documents."

Rules are presented in the Tags pane under the tag group name as shown in this example.

Tag and Coding Forms

For anyone who has the permission to view tags, the tags that display on the Document/Page Tags tabs are affiliated with the coding form you select. Your administrator may also define specific tagging rules.

Coding forms are chosen on the Record View pane in the case pane area. If you have questions on which coding form to use, contact your administrator.

Coding Form-based Rules

Your administrator may define specific rules that will apply to your review procedures when you use a specific coding form. For example, a rule may be defined to require that you add a comment in a Comment field if you apply a certain tag.

These rules apply to document (not page) tagging and must be followed as you review documents in a specific batch or case.

Permissions Required

Specific permissions govern your ability to perform many of the tasks in Eclipse. If you have any questions about your ability to perform a particular task, contact your administrator.

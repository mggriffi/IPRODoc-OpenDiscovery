Reports Overview

The Eclipse report function includes a variety of reports to assist you with review tasks, administrative duties, and case oversight. Reports are presented in an easy-to-use table format and can be exported to a .CSV file.

Before You Begin

Before you begin, ensure that you understand the reports you should generate and details of your cases. Check applicable instructions, documents or alerts in the Case Details Dashboard or contact your Eclipse administrator for instructions.

Permission Required

A specific permission is required for working with Eclipse reports. If you have any questions about performing report tasks, contact your administrator.

Log into Eclipse Web

Before You Begin

Before logging in to the Eclipse, the product installation must be completed and your Eclipse system or case administrator must define you as an Eclipse user. Your administrator will assign you to a group that is assigned to one or more cases and/or review batches. The abilities you will have are based on the permissions defined for your group in conjunction with the case you access. If you have any questions about your permissions, contact your Eclipse administrator. Log In Process

To Log in to Eclipse:

  1. Start your web browser.

  2. Enter or navigate to the URL used by your company.

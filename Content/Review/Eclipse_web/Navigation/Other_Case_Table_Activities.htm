  • IPRO Eclipse Analytics: See "Analytics Overview" if your company has this optional Eclipse feature.

Copy Record Data

To copy data for a single record in the case table:

  1. As needed, show or hide columns as explained in "Show/hide and organize columns" so that the displayed content matches your needs.

  2. Click the row of interest and then press Ctrl+C.

  3. In an appropriate file, such as a text or spreadsheet file, press Ctrl+V to paste the copied row.

    To obtain data for multiple records, see "Exporting Data for Multiple Records." To copy the data of a single field, see "Editing a Record."

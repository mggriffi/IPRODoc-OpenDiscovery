Opening a Transcript

To open a transcript:

  1. Log in to Eclipse and open the needed case or batch.

  2. Access the Document Details pane and click the Transcripts tab.

  3. If the transcript list is not visible, click Transcripts in the left pane of the tab.

  4. Open the needed folder and click the transcript of interest as shown in this figure.

  5. The transcript opens on the right side of the tab.

  6. As needed:

    • Undock and/or resize the tab.

    • Select a preferred font characteristics and/or line spacing from the Transcript toolbar.

    • Scroll in the transcript to view its contents.

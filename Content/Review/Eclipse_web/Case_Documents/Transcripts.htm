Transcripts

About Transcripts

Your administrator may include transcripts from such events as depositions, hearings, or cases, with the case you are reviewing. If you have the appropriate permission, you can view, search, and annotate transcripts as part of your review. In addition, various transcript reports can be generated.

Permissions Required

Access to transcripts is governed by a specific permission. Contact your administrator if you have questions about working with transcripts.

Getting Started

Familiarize yourself with components of the Transcripts tab, shown in the following figure and discussed in the following table.

Component

Description

Transcripts

Locate and select needed transcripts from the transcripts list. Transcripts are organized in folders according to the types defined by your administrator.

Annotations

Open the Annotations pane, which lists issues and includes an area for reviewer’s notes and navigation bar for highlighted content.

Access the annotation search tool and view/select transcript search results.

WordWheel

Use this index of words and numbers to locate specific content in the open transcript.

Reporting

Generate a report on transcript issues, notes, quick marks, or all annotations.

Transcript content pane

The body of the transcript display in the right pane. Scroll to see all content.

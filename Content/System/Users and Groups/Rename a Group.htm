Rename a Group

Complete the following steps to rename a group:

  1. Choose System from the main menu.
  2. In the left pane of the System page, click Groups.
  3. Click View/Edit for the group to display the Edit Group page.
  4. Click located to the left of the group name.
  5. Replace the current group name with the new group name.
  6. Click to save the new group name.

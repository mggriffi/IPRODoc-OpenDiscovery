Modify Contact Details

To change the details for a contact:

  1. Click the Settings icon in the top-right corner of the screen. The Settings icon is a global button that displays in every module of the platform.

  2. The System Manager opens. In the left pane of the System Manager, click Contacts.

  1. Click on the name of the contact you would like to edit. The contact profile opens on the right side of the screen.
  2. Click the Edit icon .
  3. Make needed changes and click Save.
  4. Repeat these steps to edit other contacts.
  5. Notify Media Manager users of pertinent changes.

FAQ Install

I have a physical machine and want a custom deployment of specific components. How do we do that?

Currently there is no way to accomplish this outside of running an MSI. Support and Cloud will be investigating these solutions in the future.

Who will be my install tech?

Depending on the situation, the client may not need one. If the client absolutely needs an install technician on the phone with them during the configuration portion of the install, one can be made available during business hours.

Can I do this upgrade on my own? Even over the weekend?

Absolutely! Just remember that installation assistance is not covered by our Emergency On-Call team, so any issues that happen will need to be dealt with during Business hours.

Can I still get an install tech available for my weekend install?

Yes, however weekend installs are very common, and availability of our reps is dependent on demand and their own availability. Be prepared to wait several weeks if you need to schedule the install for a weekend.

Can I have an install tech available during the VM prep stage?

No. This is because we do not provide support for this phase, as is not directly responsible for anything outside our own software. That means configuring servers and performing VM management is not covered by our support staff.

Export Image Options

This area of the form allows you to include or exclude redactions and provides a variety of options regarding the information that can be included/added to each image file’s header and/or footer. Most endorsement options apply to both TIFF and PDF; those that are unique to one type or the other will be unavailable (dimmed).

Note: This section does not apply if you selected Reference Existing Location for images in the Output Locations area.

In the export-set form, click to open the Image Options if this area of the form is not visible.

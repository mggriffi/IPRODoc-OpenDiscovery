Text Search

Text searches are as the name implies – full text searches for words or phrases in the case database fields (and corresponding images, native files, and so on).

  1. Enter any full-text search term or phrase in the Keyword field. You can also use any of the Boolean operators on the drop-down menu at the bottom-left of the Keyword field.

  2. Continue defining the advanced search by selecting additional criteria.

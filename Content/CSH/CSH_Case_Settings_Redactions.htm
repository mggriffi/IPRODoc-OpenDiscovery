Redaction Categories

To assist users in redacting image text, you can define “redaction categories” to redact specific image content. Up to 99 redaction categories can be defined. Newly created cases may include default redaction categories from templates or .CSE files.

Changes to redaction categories can be made after case review begins (except that redaction categories that have been applied cannot be deleted).

Define a Redaction Category

  1. In the Redaction Categories panel on the left side panel, click .

  2. Enter a label for the redaction category that will be meaningful to users.

  3. Colors: Select redaction and font colors.

  4. Evaluate the sample text for legibility.

  5. Permissions: If the redaction category should be restricted to certain review groups:

    1. Click the hyperlink "Add/Remove Groups" to open the Group Security dialog.

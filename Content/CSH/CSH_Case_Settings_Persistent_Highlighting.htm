Persistent Highlighting

To assist users in finding key terms in a case, you can configure “persistent highlighting” to emphasize specific document content. This highlighting appears in the Web Viewer and Text tabs. Identify the key terms that should be highlighted, whether or not terms are related and should have a common color scheme, and pertinent names for the types of highlighting being defined.

  1. In the Persistent Highlighting panel on the left side panel, click .

  2. Enter a Name for the highlighting that will be meaningful to users.

  3. Colors: Select background and font colors. Evaluate the sample text for legibility.

  4. Terms: In the Terms field, enter all terms (one per line) that belong to this persistent highlighting group.

  5. Permissions: If the highlighting should be restricted to certain review groups:

    1. Click the hyperlink "Add/Remove Groups" to open the Group Security dialog.

    2. Select needed groups and click OK.

  6. When finished, click .

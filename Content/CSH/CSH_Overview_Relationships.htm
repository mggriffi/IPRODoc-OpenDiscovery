Overview: Relationships

Relationships are based on the content of a specific field. When users select a particular document and want to view relationship details. Relationships panels presents all documents with matching field content (as in a field-specific search). There are six default panels that associate relationships with each new case:

To view the relationship information for the currently displayed document:

  1. Click the desired relationship tab.

  2. Click the pin icon in the top right corner of the relationships tab to keep it open.

  3. The document outlined in green indicates what document is being viewed.

  4. Return to the original document by clicking Back to original document.

Family

Will show documents that are related to one another, as in an email and its attachment. Family relationships are grouped by the BEGATTACH field and sorted by the BEGDOC field; you can define the details (fields and/or tag groups) to be included in the list.

Duplicates

Will show documents that are the same in content. Duplicates are grouped by the MD5HASH field and sorted by the BEGDOC field; you can define the details (fields and/or tag groups) to be included in the list.

Near Duplicates

Will show documents that are the same in content. Duplicates are grouped by the MD5HASH field and sorted by the BEGDOC field; you can define the details (fields and/or tag groups) to be included in the list.

Email Thread

Displays relationships among a family of email headers to be identified, based on their content and associated metadata. A combination of email headers and email body content is used to determine when emails belong in the same thread.

Document History

Displays history of actions such as redactions and tags applied to the selected document. Although not modifiable, this report can be secured.

Similar Documents

Displays a list of documents that contain elements similar to a selected document.

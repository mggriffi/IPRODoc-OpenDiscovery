Automatic Report Detection

The system is capable of automatically detecting and displaying custom reports in the website. This automatic detection allows you to see and run custom reports created in other applications through the website. is capable of auto detecting in and eCapture by .

To take full advantage of the Reporting system, such as customizing the display of your data or executing a report with specific SQL parameters, you must create and configure a Report Definition within the system, See Create a Report Definition for more information.

Note: Customized reports are automatically detected and appear in the reporting area under Uncategorized with a Custom Report placeholder.

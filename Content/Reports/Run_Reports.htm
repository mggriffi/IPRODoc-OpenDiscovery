Run Reports

Run a report in the Reports module as follows.

  1. On the Reports page, click the case for which the report is needed.

    1. The case-selection menu (shown in the following figure) is organized by Managing Client/Client/Code Name/Case, similar to the organization on the Case Management page.

    2. If you select System Reports, the types of reports applicable (Billing and Media) display. See System Reports for additional information.

    3. After you select a case, the types of reports applicable to the case type (Review or Processing) display. If custom reports exist at your site, they are also listed.

  3. A default preview report image appears for standard reports (excluding custom reports) such as a pie chart. For information on customizing this area, see Customize the Report Preview Image.

Log Into Reports

Before logging in, first you will need to obtain the URL and user credentials defined for System Administration, along with any additional instructions from your administrator.

To log into Reports:

  1. Open your web browser.

  2. Enter or navigate to the URL defined for Administration.

  3. On the Login screen, enter the credentials provided by your administrator.

  4. Optional: Select the Remember me option to save your login information.

  5. Click Login. Depending on your user permissions, either the Reports page or Home page displays.

  6. If you are working in , click Reports on the Home page or the main menu.

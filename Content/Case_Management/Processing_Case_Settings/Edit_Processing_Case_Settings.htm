Manage Settings for a Processing Case

You can customize an array of settings for your Processing cases within the Case Management module of . By navigating to the Case Settings work area, you can view and modify case details, as well as configure settings related to document ingestion, filtering, and imaging. Many of the most common processing settings can be found in , with additional settings available in . When you modify settings in , they are simultaneously updated in Streaming Discovery options, and vice-versa.

Note: These processing case settings apply to Streaming Discovery options only. Standard Discovery options must be configured within the eCapture Controller.

What's New in Self-Service

As of version 2018.5.0, Administrators have the ability to assign a specific System permission, Self-Service Upload, at the group level allowing the group’s users to Upload a File for Processing Later. Data may be processed into those Cases to which the group has been assigned.

The case level permission, Self-Service Upload & Process, must be assigned for a group whose users will require the function Upload a File to Process into Review.
